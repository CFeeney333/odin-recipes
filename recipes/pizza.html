Pizza

Description

A nice easy Italian style cheese pizza with a thick crust. This recipe makes 2 12" pizzas enough for your family and friends to share!!! cringe

  • Servings: 6
  • Yield: 2 12-inch pizzas

Ingredients

  • Pizza Sauce

    • 1/2 can CONTADINA Tomato Paste
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, crushed
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil, crushed
    • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

  • Pizza Crust

    • 3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour, or more as needed
    • 2 envelopes FLEISCHMANN'S Pizza Crust Yeast
    • 1 tablespoon sugar
    • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
    • 1 1/3 cups very warm water (120 degrees F to 130 degrees F)
    • 1/3 cup oil

  • Toppings

    • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, or more to taste

Directions

  1. For sauce: Combine all sauce ingredients with 1/2 cup water in a medium bowl; set aside for flavors to develop while making crust. Freeze remaining paste.

  2. For crusts: Combine 2 cups of flour with the dry yeast, sugar and salt. Add the water and oil and mix until well blended (about 1 minute). Gradually add enought remaining flour slowly, until a soft, sticky dough ball is formed.

  3. Knead for about 4 minutes, on a floured surface, until dough is smooth and elastic. Add more flour, if needed. (If using RapidRise Yeast, let dough rest, covered, for 10 minutes.)

  4. Divide dough in half. Pat each half (with floured hands) into a 12-inch greased pizza pan OR roll dough to fit pans.

  5. Bake for 18 to 20 minutes until crusts are browned and cheese is bubbly. For best results, rotate pizza pans between top and bottom oven racks halfway through baking.
