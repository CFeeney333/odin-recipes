Pizza

Description

A nice easy Italian style cheese pizza with a thick crust. This recipe makes 2 12" pizzas enough for your family and friends to share!!! cringe

Ingredients

  • Pizza Sauce

    • 1/2 can CONTADINA Tomato Paste
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, crushed
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil, crushed
    • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

  • Pizza Crust

    • 3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour, or more as needed
    • 2 envelopes FLEISCHMANN'S Pizza Crust Yeast
    • 1 tablespoon sugar
    • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
    • 1 1/3 cups very warm water (120 degrees F to 130 degrees F)
    • 1/3 cup oil

  • Toppings

    • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, or more to taste
